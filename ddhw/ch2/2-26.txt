positive logic NAND is negetive logic NOR and vice versa

positive logic NAND
A   B   |   F
-------------
L   L   |   H
L   H   |   H
H   L   |   H
H   H   |   L

positive logic NOR
A   B   |   F
-------------
L   L   |   H
L   H   |   L
H   L   |   L
H   H   |   L

negetive logic NAND
A   B   |   F
-------------
L   L   |   H
L   H   |   L
H   L   |   L
H   H   |   L

negetive logic NOR
A   B   |   F
-------------
L   L   |   H
L   H   |   H
H   L   |   H
H   H   |   L

comparing truth table of negetive logic NOR with positive logic NAND and
comparing truth table of negetive logic NAND with positive logic NOR
proves it.
